About me and my blog

Hi, my name is Marice and I am a hairdresser/barber. I started my apprenticeship when i was 18, but i joined my first competition when i was 17 and still in school. I became a certified hairdresser when i was 20, and started to work as a barber when i was 25. I like to learn new things, i love exploring and creating beautiful designs with colors, and i work the best under pressure, so this job suits me well. I decided to start this blog for people that might want to become a hairdresser or a barber, and want to know what it's like. We need more hairdressers and barbers, and i hope this blog will spike a bigger interest in people. This blog is also for hairdressers wondering about starting their own buisness, or people that would just like to follow and laugh with us!
